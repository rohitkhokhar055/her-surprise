The Last Page

This page should end softly.

Not with another question. Not with another attempt to hold onto something that has already moved on.

So let me say the things I should have said more clearly a long time ago.

Forgive me for the past. For the things I handled badly, for the ways I held on too long, and for every moment where my feelings became more of a burden than they should have been.

I did love you. Really. Maybe not always perfectly, maybe not always in the right way, but what I felt was real.

And because it was real, I think the right thing to do now is to leave with honesty instead of noise.

I know you are not where you once were with me. I know your life has moved forward, and I can feel that I no longer belong in the place I kept trying to return to.

So this is me accepting it properly.

Be happy with whoever is beside you now. Be loved well. Be understood gently. Be chosen in the way you always deserved to be.

Enjoy your day. Enjoy your life. I mean that sincerely.

And I will try not to text you again or disturb your peace. Not because I never cared, but because I did, and I think caring should also know when to step back.

You will always remain someone important in a part of me. But I do not want to keep turning memory into interruption.

So this is where I leave it.

Take care of yourself, Ishu.

I loved you truly. Now I just hope life is kind to you, even if it is no longer with me.
